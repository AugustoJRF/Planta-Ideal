The Ideal Plant

Each species of plant requires a specific set of conditions to develop in a healthy manner, such as the amount of light and water, for example.

How to discover your ideal plant?

The three statements at the end of the page help in selecting which of the 15 presented plants will best fit the environment you have available. If you identify with the condition, mark "Yes", if the condition does not correspond, mark "No".

After answering the three statements, click on "Check the plants".

Note: There are several other conditions that directly influence the healthy development of a plant, such as temperature, climate, and soil type.
